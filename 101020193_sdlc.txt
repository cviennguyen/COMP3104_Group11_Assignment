What is DevOps?
- combines software development with IT operation creating the portmanteau "DevOps"
- originated from agile software development
- shortens and creates fewer redundancies in the systems development life cycle (SDLC)
- key principles of shared ownership, workflow automation and rapid feedback

CI/CD Pipeline
- CI (Continuous integration): frequent merging of small change to the main branch
	- automated builds with tools to spot errors easier
- CD (Continuous Delivery): short development cycles at high speed and frequency
- CD (Continuous Deployment): new features delivered frequently
